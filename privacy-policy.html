Privacy Policy — Streaml ConnectIN LinkedIn Message Assist

Last updated: 2025-08-13

Streaml ConnectIN LinkedIn Message Assist is a Chrome extension that helps users send a saved default message to profiles on linkedin.com. This extension is unofficial and not affiliated with or endorsed by LinkedIn Corporation.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

The extension stores only two keys in the browser’s local storage:

  • popupMode — whether the assistant is “Open” or “Closed”.

These values stay on the user’s device and are not transmitted to our servers or third parties by this extension.

Permissions & Purpose

  • tabs — open linkedin.com profile pages in background tabs.
  • scripting — inject a small script to open the Message composer and send the saved default message.
  • storage — save the two settings listed above.
  • host_permissions: https://www.linkedin.com/ — restricts operation to LinkedIn only.
  • externally_connectable — allows a user’s Streaml page (on allowed origins) to send a list of profile URLs to the extension. This is data pushed by the user; the extension does not contact any remote servers on its own.

External Services

This extension does not use analytics, advertising, or external APIs. It does not transmit or receive any personal data other than the URLs you explicitly send from your Streaml page to the extension.

Security

  • Data stays on the user’s device via chrome.storage.local.
  • Communication from Streaml relies on Chrome’s externally_connectable security model which restricts allowed origins via the extension manifest.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • Settings remain on the device until the user uninstalls the extension or clears extension data via browser settings.
  • Uninstalling the extension deletes all stored data.

Children’s Privacy

This extension is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will update the “Last updated” date above when changes are made.
